Thank you for participating in a project on colour perception. This research aims to investigate the effect of language on colour perception.

Participants were separated into two groups which would each perform 2 tasks within a repeated measures design. One experiment required students to undergo a verbal interference task whereas the other did not. Participants were not told in advance what colours or what interference task they would undergo.

If you experience discomfort (eg eye strain, wrist fatigue, or headache) completing the visual search task as it is administered on a laptop screen, you may contact the researchers using email addresses provided above to contact a relevant medical service.
